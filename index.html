Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online using the “Reserve Now” button on our website, or send us a message through text or Facebook. We’ll confirm your appointment and answer any questions you have about your pet's needs.

What areas do you serve?

We’re based in Hayward, WI and currently serve clients in Hayward and surrounding Northwoods communities. Not sure if we come to you? Send us a quick message and we’ll let you know!

What services do you offer?

We offer full-service grooming including baths, haircuts, nail trims, deshedding, ear cleaning, flea & tick treatment, and more. You can check out the full list of services with cute icons right on our website.

Do you groom all breeds?

Yes! We welcome dogs of all breeds, sizes, and coat types. If your dog has special needs, just let us know when you book—we’re happy to accommodate.

How should I prepare my dog?

Please have your pup freshly walked and allow them a potty break before your appointment. Let us know if your dog has any health issues or sensitivities so we can make grooming as stress-free as possible.
